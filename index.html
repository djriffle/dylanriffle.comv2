E. Colight

The result of exploring organic light sources. The E. Colight creates light using an E. Coli colony in a liquid medium. It uses a less odorous strain of E. Coli genetically modified with jellyfish and yeast DNA to produce light while creating a banana-like scent, which reduces the presence of E. Coli's natural smell that previously inhibited its ability to be incorporated into design.
